Hyperskill is an online learning platform that helps people from all over the world learn new skills in Computer Science and beyond. We firmly believe in a holistic approach to learning that allows Hyperskill students to apply theoretical knowledge right to practice while building their own working apps.

Regular exercise is key to mastering new skills. With adjustable reminders, daily practice tasks, and a learning streak tracker, My Hyperskill app offers a convenient way to study on your mobile phone wherever and whenever you like.
